Zero
Associação Zero wanted to renew its identity, aiming to make it more coherent and modern, especially among young people. The rebranding reflects its commitment to promoting the values of sustainability in Portuguese society. We assigned distinct colors to its seven core values, making them easier to recognize and reinforcing their meaning when addressing the respective themes.
Group project - Carolina Martins, Maria Chimeno and Salomé Vasconcelos
